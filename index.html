WHAT IS NCC

• The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a Voluntary Youth Military Organization in India under the Ministry of Defense with its Headquarters at New Delhi.

• The organization recruits cadets from high schools, colleges and universities all over India. The Cadets are given basic military training in small arms, parades, personality development and are involved into social service.
